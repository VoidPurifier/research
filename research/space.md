# Space Research

## Interstellar Comet 3I/ATLAS (Jul 2025)
- Third interstellar object ever found
- Confirmed to be 10-12 billion years old
- Webb telescope found it has 30x more deuterium (heavy hydrogen) than solar system comets
- Suggests it formed in a very cold environment during the universe's "cosmic noon"

## "Black Hole Stars" Confirmed (Jun 2026)
- Webb captured deepest spectrum yet of a "Little Red Dot" (GLIMPSE-17775)
- Found 40+ spectral signatures supporting that these mysterious red objects are supermassive black holes wrapped in dense gas cocoons
- Explains their unusual infrared glow

## Pink Planet Has Salt Clouds (Jun 2026)
- JWST revealed GJ 504b's atmosphere contains clouds made of salt
- First direct evidence of salt clouds on an exoplanet
- Cold gas giant, nicknamed the "Pink Planet," sits near the planet-brown dwarf boundary

## Dormant Giant Black Hole Found (Jun 2026)
- Most distant dormant black hole ever: 6 billion solar masses
- Located 10 billion light-years away in galaxy MRG-M0138
- Broke the previous distance record by 15x
- Shows how black holes can shut down star formation in host galaxies

## Nancy Grace Roman Telescope (Jun 2026)
- NASA's next flagship observatory arrived at Kennedy Space Center
- Targeting August 30 launch on Falcon Heavy
- Will find hundreds of thousands of new exoplanets
- Test starlight-blocking coronagraph technology for future Earth-like planet imaging
